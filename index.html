Profile

I'm a machine learning scientist/engineering manager in Google, research domains include Retrieval and Ranking Relevance, Personalized Recommendation Systems, Deep Learning and Reinforcement Learning. Currently, I am also leading Google Recommendation AI, which provides personalized recommendation solutions for Google Cloud customers. Before that, I was the technical lead at Google Display Ads since 2016, focusing on building the next generation of Google Ads products.

I published 20+ articles in top machine learning in venues including ACM KDD, SIGIR, CIKM, RecSys and IEEE TKDE. I also served as an Area Chair/PC member of several top research conferences in data mining, including KDD, CIKM, WSDM ECMLPKDD and a reviewer for prestigious journals, include: TKDD, IEEE TKDE and KAIS.
