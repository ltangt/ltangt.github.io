RESEARCH INTERESTS

Broadly interested in data and information analysis including data mining, machine learning and information retrieval techniques. More specifically, I focus on the event mining that discovers and summarizes the hidden relations of the event data, streaming data, time series data and textual data. Practically, it includes the applications in automatic system management and service computing, which aim to help the system administrator better understand and maintain modern large and dynamic IT infrastructures. I am also interested in computational advertising, which includes optimizing the recommendation models and offline evaluation for online advertising and multi-arm bandit algorithms.
